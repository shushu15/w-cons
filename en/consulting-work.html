The list of operations by consulting company:

  • Negotiations with a customer, determination of all requirements to created system, or audit of existing one. Preparing Terms of Reference (TOR) for system design and installation.
  • Specification preparing (TKO). Defining of equipment budget.
  • Designing of system – the stage of project (P).
  • Tendering process for design – the stage of working documents (WD).
  • Expertise of the project.
  • Tendering process for equipment supply, materials and software.
  • Tendering process for equipment installation.
  • Monitoring of supply of equipment, materials and software.
  • Monitoring of installation and commissioning, training of personnel in accordance with Terms of Reference and implementation timeline.
  • Acceptance of the system in operation on behalf of customer.
  • Tendering process for system service (if necessary organization of outsourcing service for its operation).
  • If necessary, conducting the same actions for expansion and upgrade of this system.
