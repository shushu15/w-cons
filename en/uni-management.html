Functions of technical supervision.

Incoordination in actions of project members might emerge at all stages of project implementation, in case of realization of complex solutions, as well as in between stages. So in frames of the whole project, the supervising function becomes crucial, it is possible to avoid small faults (equipment replacement etc.) as well as more serious problems (failure in some parts of work).

We are ready to take over the functions of technical control executing on behalf of customer. In this case, we have control over scope and quality of work execution at all stages of the project in accordance with the technical, financial and time characteristics.
